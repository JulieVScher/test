About Me

I am an artistic and analytical UI/UX designer and Board Certified Behaviour Analyst (BCBA). My knowledge of UI/UX methodologies, in combination with my deep understanding of the principles of human behaviour, allow me to create enjoyable digital experiences to keep users engaged. I conduct research, empathize with user pain points, and ideate to create elegant solutions for complex problems. With over 10 years of experience working with individuals with autism, and other developmental challenges, I strive to ensure that digital spaces are accessible and inclusive for everyone.

I employ design tools, such as Figma and Adobe XD, to create wireframes and high fidelity prototypes. I can effectively collaborate with design teams, clients, and programmers. I have experience with web development and enjoy using HTML, CSS, and Javascript to build responsive websites.
